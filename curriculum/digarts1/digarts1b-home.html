CTE Digital Arts 1B
Welcome Back.
This course is all about creating design work that communicates clearly and looks professional. You will build real skills in logo design, branding, layout, and marketing graphics using Adobe Creative Cloud. Work follows a weekly rhythm: lessons on Monday, demos on Tuesday, and lab time Wednesday through Friday with most work due on Friday. Stay organized, manage your files, and ask questions early.
