About Your Teacher
Welcome to Photography & Digital Arts.
I’m Mr. Silva, and this course is all about exploring your creativity through the lens. I’ve been a professional photographer for over 15 years and co-own a wedding, event, and portrait photography company with my wife.
In this class you’ll work on creative projects and have the chance to check out cameras to document school events, sports, and everyday campus life, capturing energy, perspective, and personality. We’ll challenge how you see the familiar and help you develop your own visual style.
Be ready to experiment, express, and grow.
