About Your Teacher
I’m Mr. Silva, a photographer and visual arts educator with over 15 years of professional experience. I’ve worked across the United States, Japan, and Mexico, shooting everything from weddings and portraits to commercial campaigns, motorsports, and the music industry. I co-own a photography and media production company with my wife Laura, who is also a photography educator and industry professional.
My connection to photography started with a single camera: a Nikon F3 film camera given to me by my uncle before he passed. I still shoot with it today. I call it The Spirit Box, because it reminds me that image-making can honor memory, identity, and purpose. That mindset is at the core of everything I try to bring into the classroom.
In this class you’ll work on real creative projects and have the chance to check out cameras to document school events, sports, and everyday campus life. We’ll push you to be intentional about what you photograph and why. Be ready to experiment, express, and grow.
