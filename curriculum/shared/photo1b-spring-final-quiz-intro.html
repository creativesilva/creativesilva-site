About This Quiz
What You Are About to Take
This is the Spring Photography Final. It pulls from everything we covered this semester: the exposure triangle, lenses, composition, lighting, color, post-processing, and project workflow.
Read each question carefully. Answer with what you know. Use the notebook you built during the lecture review.
Format
Quick Look at the Test
Multiple Choice
Every question has four answer choices. Pick the one that is correct.
One Attempt
You get one shot. Take your time. Double-check before you submit.
Auto-Submit
If a timer is set, Canvas turns it in for you when the time runs out.
Shuffled
Question order and answer order are shuffled. Your test looks different than your neighbor’s.
The Big Rule
Open Book = Notebook Only
The only resource you can use is the notebook you wrote during the lecture review.
No phones. No browsers. No PDFs. No notes from a friend. No AI. Hands on your keyboard and eyes on the screen.

Este es el Examen Final de Fotografía de Primavera. Toma de todo lo que cubrimos este semestre: el triángulo de exposición, los lentes, la composición, la iluminación, el color, la postproducción y el flujo de trabajo.
