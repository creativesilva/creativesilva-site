CTE Digital Arts 2B
Level Up Your Design.
Welcome to Digital Arts 2B. This course is for students who are ready to level up their design work and create pieces that look polished, intentional, and client ready. You will build stronger skills in branding systems, logo development, layout design, and marketing graphics while making choices that improve visual hierarchy, readability, and audience impact. Each week follows a steady rhythm: lessons on Monday, demos on Tuesday, and lab time Wednesday through Friday with most work due on Friday. Stay organized, manage your files, and ask questions early.
