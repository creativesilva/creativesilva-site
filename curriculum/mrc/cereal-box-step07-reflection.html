Reflect. Learn. Grow.
PROJECT WRAP / 01 / 05
Look Back at What You Built
You finished your cereal box. Now it is time to think about how the project went. This is the part real designers do at the end of every job.
You will fill out a reflection Word doc. You will write about your design choices, how you and your partner worked together, and what you learned while creating your brand.
Be honest. Be specific. This is the easiest grade in the module, but only if you actually reflect.
HOW TO COMPLETE THIS ASSIGNMENT / 02 / 05
Three Steps to Finish
Download the reflection doc. Answer every question in complete sentences. Save and submit.

PREVIEW OF THE PROMPTS / 03 / 05
Six Things to Reflect On
Scroll through. These are the prompts the reflection doc will ask you to answer. Write in full sentences. No one-word answers.
Prompt 1: My Design Choices
What design choices did you make on your panels? Why those colors, fonts, or images?
Prompt 2: What Went Right
What part of the project went the best? Why do you think it worked?
Prompt 3: What I’d Change
If you could redo one thing on your box, what would it be and why?
Prompt 4: Partner Wins
What did you and your partner do really well together? Name something specific.
Prompt 5: What Was Hard
What was the hardest part of the project? How did you handle it?
Prompt 6: Real-World Connect
How is what you did the same as what a real package designer does on the job?
SAVE IT THE RIGHT WAY / 04 / 05
File Naming
Save your reflection to your school OneDrive. Use this exact file name pattern:
CerealBoxReflection_LastName_FirstName.docx
Example: CerealBoxReflection_Silva_Chris.docx
