Your Final Sketch

CHOOSE YOUR BEST / 01 / 03
Pick the Winning Idea
Look back at your three thumbnails and the feedback from Mr. Silva. Choose the one idea that is the strongest and most exciting to build.
You can also combine the best parts of two thumbnails into one stronger idea. Designers do this all the time.
DRAW IT CLEAN / 02 / 03
One Polished Final Sketch
Now draw your chosen idea one more time, larger and cleaner. This is your final sketch, the plan you will follow in Photoshop.
Make it neat: clean lines, clear shapes, and the colors you plan to use. Add notes about your fonts or colors off to the side if it helps.
This sketch does not need to be perfect art. It needs to be a clear map of your new logo.

PHOTOGRAPH AND TURN IN / 03 / 03
Snap It and Submit
Lay all of your sketches flat in good light. Use your phone to take clear, sharp photos of your three thumbnails and your final sketch.
Tips for a good photo: fill the frame with the paper, hold the phone straight above the page, and keep your hand’s shadow off the drawing.
Turn your photos in to Step 2 in Canvas. Name your file:
FirstName LastInitial - Logo Sketch

What to Submit
Clear photos of all three thumbnails plus your one polished final sketch.
