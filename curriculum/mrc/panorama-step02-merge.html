Panorama Project  •  Step 2
Merge in Photoshop
Stitch your photos into one wide image.
OPEN PHOTOMERGE / 01 / 04
Start Photomerge
Open Photoshop. Go to File > Automate > Photomerge. This is the tool that stitches your photos into one panorama.
You will run it twice: once for your campus photos and once for your home photos.
BUILD THE PANORAMA / 02 / 04
Choose and Blend
Set up the merge in the Photomerge window. Scroll through each step.
Merge 01
Keep Layout on Auto
In the Photomerge window, leave Layout set to Auto. It works best for most panoramas.
Merge 02
Browse and Select
Click Browse. Go to your RAW Images folder and select the 4 to 5 .CR3 files for one panorama. Click OK.
Merge 03
Check the Boxes
Turn on Blend Images Together, Vignette Removal, and Geometric Distortion Correction.
Merge 04
Fill the Edges
Also check Content Aware Fill Transparent Areas to fill the empty edges for you, then click OK. Watch out: if the fill looks fake or messy, you can crop those edges off later, or run Photomerge again with this box unchecked.
Merge 05
Let It Build
Photoshop opens each RAW file through Camera Raw, lines them up, and blends them into one layered panorama. Give it a minute.

CLEAN IT UP / 03 / 04
Flatten, Crop, Adjust
Flatten the layers into one image: Layer > Flatten Image.
Crop off any uneven or fake-looking edges with the Crop tool. If you left Content Aware Fill on and it looks great, you may not need to crop much at all.
Adjust your photo with the Camera Raw Filter (Filter > Camera Raw Filter) or adjustment layers. Fix the exposure, contrast, and color until it looks great.
Now do this whole process again for your other panorama (your home one).
SAVE AND SUBMIT / 04 / 04
Save as JPG and Submit
After editing, flatten your image with Layer > Flatten Image if you have not already. PSD files are very large, so you only need to keep a PSD if you want one for yourself.
You turn in a JPG, not a PSD. Use File > Save a Copy, choose JPG, full size, and save it to your Panorama folder. Name each one by its location:
FirstName LastInitial - Panorama Campus.jpg  and  FirstName LastInitial - Panorama Home.jpg
Submit at least two panorama JPGs to Step 2 in Canvas: at least one from campus and at least one taken off campus without a tripod. Proud of more than one? Submit those too.
