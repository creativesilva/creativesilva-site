Panorama Project  •  Step 3
Reflection
Look back at what you made.
HOW IT WORKS / 01 / 02
Answer Four Questions
A reflection helps you learn from your work. Open the reflection document inside your Panorama project folder (the one you downloaded at the start). Answer all four questions in complete sentences.
Question 01
What Worked
What worked well in your panoramas? What are you proud of?
Question 02
What Did Not
What did not work, or what was tricky during shooting or merging?
Question 03
Do Differently
What would you do differently next time to make a better panorama?
Question 04
Favorite and Dream
What did you like most about this project? And if you could shoot a panorama of anywhere in the world, where would it be and why?
