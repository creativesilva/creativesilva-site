Digital Arts 1A  •  Cereal Box Project  •  Step 06
Social Media Content
Take your cereal brand to Instagram.
STEP 06 OVERVIEW / 01 / 08
Sell Your Cereal Online
Your cereal box is built. Now you sell it online. Real brands do not stop at the box. They post on Instagram to get people excited.
In this step you make two Instagram pieces for your cereal brand: a short animated Story and a square Post. You will build them in Canva.
Use the same brand you and your partner made: the same name, logo, colors, fonts, and mascot from Step 05.
THE TOOL YOU WILL USE / 02 / 08
Build It in Canva
Canva is free with your school Google login. It has ready-made Instagram sizes, animation tools, and fonts. You do not need any other app for this step.
Canva
Your one tool for both pieces
→ Log in: Go to canva.com and click Log in with Google. Use your school account.
→ Start: Click Create a design, then search Instagram Story or Instagram Post to get the right size.
→ Animate: Click any element, then Animate to make text and logos move.
→ Upload: Use the Uploads tab to bring in your logo, mascot, and box images from Step 05.
New to Canva? Watch the free lessons at canva.com/designschool.
TWO DELIVERABLES / 03 / 08
You Will Make Two Pieces
1. Story
Animated Instagram Story
Export as MP4 video.
Size: 1080 x 1920 (tall 9:16) • Length: 5 to 15 seconds • Must have at least one thing that moves: animated text, a logo reveal, or a transition.
2. Post
Static Instagram Post
Export as JPG image.
Size: 1080 x 1080 (square 1:1) • One clean image that shows your cereal, your logo, and one clear message.
PRO TIP / 04 / 08
Give People a Call to Action
A call to action tells the viewer what to do next. It is short and easy to see. Both your Story and your Post need one.
Good examples: “Try it today.” • “Find it at your store.” • “Follow for more.” • “New flavor, out now.”
STAY ON BRAND / 05 / 08
Use Your Own Cereal Brand
Your Story and Post must match the cereal box you built. Pull these straight from your Step 05 files so it all looks like one brand.
Same Logo
Use the exact logo from your box. Upload it to Canva.
Same Colors
Use the same HEX colors from your Step 03 doc.
Same Fonts
Pick fonts in Canva that match your box style.
Same Mascot
If your box has a mascot, put it in your posts too.
HOW TO COMPLETE STEP 06 / 06 / 08
Follow These Steps in Canva
01. Open Canva and Upload Your Brand
Log in to Canva with your school Google account. Use the Uploads tab to add your logo, mascot, and any box images from Step 05.
02. Build the Instagram Story
Create a new Instagram Story design (1080 x 1920). Add your cereal name, logo, one image, and a call to action.
03. Add Animation to the Story
Click an element, then Animate. Make your text or logo move. Keep the video between 5 and 15 seconds.
04. Build the Instagram Post
Create a new Instagram Post design (1080 x 1080). Make one clean square image with your cereal, your logo, and one clear message.
05. Export the Story as MP4
Click Share, then Download, then pick MP4 Video. Name it LastName_Cereal_Story.mp4.
06. Export the Post as JPG
Click Share, then Download, then pick JPG. Name it LastName_Cereal_Post.jpg.
07. Submit Both Files to Canvas
Upload your MP4 and your JPG to this Canvas assignment in one submission. Check that the video plays and the image opens.

WHAT TO SUBMIT / 08 / 08
Upload Your MP4 and Your JPG
Upload both your Instagram Story MP4 and your Instagram Post JPG to this Canvas assignment in one submission. Two files, two formats. After you submit, move on to Step 07: Project Reflection.
