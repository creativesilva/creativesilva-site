Digital Arts 1A  •  Cereal Box Project  •  Step 04
Concept Sketch
Sketch your box sides by hand. Sketch first. Design smarter.
PLAN BEFORE YOU CLICK / 01 / 06
Why You Sketch First
In this step, you sketch a plan for the panels you are designing. This helps you figure out your ideas before you open Adobe Illustrator.
You and your partner already picked colors, fonts, and art style in Step 03. Now you sketch your three assigned sides so all six sides of the box look like one brand.
Real designers sketch every project before opening any software. You will too.
DESIGNER ROLES / 02 / 06
Who Sketches What
You split the six box sides between the two of you. Each designer takes three sides.
Designer 1
Front, Top & Left
Your three sides. Front carries the name, mascot, and tagline. Top is the lid. Left holds a small logo and tagline.
Designer 2
Back, Bottom & Right
Your three sides. Back tells why your cereal is special. Bottom holds the barcode. Right holds the nutrition facts.
HOW TO COMPLETE THIS ASSIGNMENT / 03 / 06
Three Steps to Finish
Check your assigned sides. Sketch them in your book. Scan to one PDF and submit.
01
Check Your Sides
Look at the Designer Roles card. Confirm which three sides are yours before you draw anything.
02
Sketch Your Three Sides
Sketch each side on its own sketchbook page. Show where pictures, text, and logo go.
03
Approval + PDF Submit
Show the teacher your sketches for approval. Scan to one PDF. Upload to Canvas.
MAKE YOUR SKETCH USEFUL / 04 / 06
Sketching Tips
Map It Out
Show where every piece goes: name, mascot, tagline, bullets, logo. Use boxes to block out space.
Add Color Notes
Use colored pencils or write the HEX codes from your group palette next to each area.
Talk to Your Partner
Check that your sketches feel like the same brand as your partner’s sides. If not, adjust.
Clear as a Map
It does not have to be perfect, but it must be clear. You will use this as a map in Illustrator.
IMPORTANT / 05 / 06
Get Teacher Approval Before Step 05
Before you open Adobe Illustrator, show me your sketches in person.
No approval means no Illustrator. This step saves you from designing in the wrong direction for hours.
WHAT TO SUBMIT / 06 / 06
One Clean PDF
When your sketches are done and the teacher has approved, scan or take clear photos of your three sketchbook pages. Save them as one PDF and upload to this Canvas assignment.
Make sure the photo is bright and easy to read. When you finish, click the Done button to move to Step 05.
