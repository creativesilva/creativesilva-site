Camera Modes  •  Step 2
Cull and Edit
Pick and polish your best eight.
CULL YOUR BEST / 01 / 03
Pick the Best Eight
Look through everything you shot. Pick your best eight: two shallow and two deep from AV, and two freeze and two blur from TV.
Choosing your strongest shots and dropping the rest is called culling. Keep the photos that show each effect the clearest.
EDIT YOUR PHOTOS / 02 / 03
Make Them Pop
Open your eight photos in Photoshop or Adobe Camera Raw. Because they are RAW files, you have lots of room to fix them.
Adjust the exposure, contrast, and color, and crop for a stronger frame. Keep it looking natural and real, do not overdo it.
SAVE AND SUBMIT / 03 / 03
Submit Eight JPGs
Save each photo as a JPG: File > Save a Copy, choose JPG, full size. Name them so your AV and TV shots are clear, like:
FirstName LastInitial - AV Shallow 1.jpg
Submit all eight JPGs to Step 2 in Canvas: four AV and four TV.
