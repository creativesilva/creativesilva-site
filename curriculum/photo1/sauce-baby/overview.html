Commercial Photography Final
Food and product photography campaign for Sauce Baby.
Module Overview
Create a Commercial Image Campaign
For this spring final, you will create a food and product photography project for Sauce Baby. You will plan your idea, photograph the product, build contact sheets, edit your strongest images, and reflect on your final work.
Your goal is to make images that feel useful for social media, advertising, and brand content. You are not just taking pictures. You are creating a small commercial photography campaign.
