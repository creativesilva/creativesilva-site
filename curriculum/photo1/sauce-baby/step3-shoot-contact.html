Assignment Overview
Photograph Your Commercial Campaign
For this assignment, you will photograph the images for your commercial photography final and create contact sheets that show your full shooting process. Your contact sheets should prove that you explored different ideas, compositions, camera angles, lighting choices, and product styling options.
You are not turning in only your best images yet. You are showing your work like a photographer: many attempts, many variations, and clear evidence of creative decision-making.
Shooting Goal
Create a Strong Set of RAW Images
Minimum Images
Shoot enough images to create complete contact sheets. Your work should show variety, not just repeats.
RAW Capture
Photograph in RAW so you have more control when editing your final images later.
Commercial Purpose
Your photos should feel useful for advertising, social media, product promotion, or brand content.

Camera Expectations
Shoot With Intention
Manual Mode
Control aperture, shutter speed, and ISO. Do not rely on automatic settings.
Lighting
Use light intentionally. Watch for shadows, highlights, reflections, and mood.
Composition
Try different angles, crops, backgrounds, props, and product placement.
No Phone Cameras
Use the classroom camera workflow unless your teacher gives you different directions.
What to Photograph
Show Variety in Your Shoot
01
Hero Shots
Create strong main images where the product or subject is clearly the focus.
02
Detail Shots
Photograph close-up details such as texture, ingredients, labels, surfaces, or important visual features.
03
Composition Tests
Try vertical, horizontal, close, wide, low angle, overhead, centered, and off-center compositions.
04
Lighting Tests
Show that you experimented with different light directions, brightness levels, and shadow choices.

Expectativas de Cámara

Composición
